Rookie Vision

Introduction

In today's world, many children are given tablets at the age of two. Rookie Vision is a free, accessible website that takes images and determines what it is! This can be fun to both children as little as 3 to a grown adult! Critical thinking and learning to use technology are fundamental skills to demanding jobs such as a Data Scientist.

Rookie Vision recognizes facial expressions of humans, and can be used to teach
common sense and real-life situations.

About Us

On the day of HackUMassIX, the group was formed. We had no expectations or prior knowledge
of ideas and projects. After hours of brainstorming between crazy ideas from moving pillows,
U-card finder, and drones, we finally decided on creating a website using Google's API.
